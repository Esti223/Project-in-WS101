    Lasagne is a traditional Italian dish made with alternating layers of wide, flat pasta sheets, rich meat sauce, béchamel sauce, and grated cheese. The pasta is typically layered and baked until golden and bubbly. Lasagne variations can include different types of meat (such as beef, pork, or lamb), vegetables, and various cheeses. This hearty and flavorful dish has become popular worldwide and is often associated with comfort food, offering a satisfying combination of textures and tastes.

Butternut pumpkin is rich in essential nutrients such as vitamin A, vitamin C, potassium, and fiber. It is commonly used in various culinary applications, including soups, stews, roasted dishes, and purees.
